Key Concepts Reference

🧬

DNA Structure

Double helix · nucleotides (sugar + phosphate + base) · A-T and G-C base pairs · antiparallel strands

📋

Transcription

DNA → mRNA · occurs in nucleus · RNA polymerase · A→U, T→A, G→C, C→G substitutions

⚙️

Translation

mRNA → protein · occurs at ribosome · tRNA brings amino acids · codons read in triplets

🔄

Central Dogma

DNA → RNA → Protein · information flows in one direction (normally)

⚡

Mutations

Substitution · insertion · deletion · frameshift mutations alter reading frame; nonsense mutations create stop codons

🔁

DNA Replication

Semi-conservative · each strand is a template · DNA polymerase · occurs before cell division
